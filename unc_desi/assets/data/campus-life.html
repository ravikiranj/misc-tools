Campus Life

UNC Chapel Hill has a very beautiful campus and it's insanely huge. Chapel Hill pretty much has the university itself and nothing else other than that. So, it is a peaceful little town and will help your studies by taking all the city distractions out of your way. In order to survive the gruelling M.S or Ph.D. that you are pursuing, it's important to know about some of the important aspects such as Food, Shopping, Sports, Transportation and the Music scene here at Chapel Hill.
